## Codebase Patterns
- Keep marketplace review additive first; surface better evidence before tightening approval gates.
- Treat compiled evidence as a separate authoritative layer from raw proof verification.
- Narrative may help reviewers, but the marketplace must never let it override authoritative compiled control results.
- Prefer submission detail/list improvements before broad arena/autopilot propagation.
- Reviewer-facing compiled evidence views should compact list/detail payloads and treat reference-only attachments as unverified/unavailable rather than as provided-but-invalid authoritative reports.
