## Codebase Patterns
- Provenance is not enough for privacy claims; privacy assurance needs prevention + receipts + reviewer-facing explanation.
- Prefer fail-closed enforcement over best-effort observation.
- Keep privacy receipts explicit and signed instead of asking reviewers to infer posture from raw telemetry.
- Make the proof report state both what **is** proven and what is **not** proven.
