## Codebase Patterns
- Build trust with signed policy + runner + reviewer artifacts, not ambient configuration or screenshots.
- Prefer fail-closed verification and marketplace gating over advisory-only reporting.
- Keep reviewer outputs honest about what is and is not proven.
- Expand assurance without regressing canonical proof-production reliability.
